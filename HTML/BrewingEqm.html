MANUAL MACHINES

Pour-over Dripper
110.99$

A "pour over dripper" is a specialized coffee brewing device used in the "pour over" method, where hot water is manually poured over coffee grounds placed in a filter within the dripper, allowing the brewed coffee to drip slowly into a cup.

French Press
120$

The French Press is a cylindrical pot with a plunger and built-in filter screen that presses hot water through ground coffee: that's the simple beauty of the French press, creating an earthy, rich taste in your daily cup of coffee.

Moka Pot
130$

A moka pot is a stovetop coffee maker that uses steam pressure to brew coffee. It's a staple of Italian coffee culture and is also popular in Latin America and Australia.

Aeropress
100$

The AeroPress is a manual coffee maker that brews coffee by forcing water and coffee grounds through a filter. It's lightweight and portable, making it a good choice for traveling.

Cold Brew Maker
110$

A cold brew coffee maker steeps coffee grounds in water for an extended period of time to create a concentrate that can be diluted with milk or water.
